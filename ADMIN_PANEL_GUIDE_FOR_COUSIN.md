## üöÄ Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the Admin Panel

1. Go to: `https://your-website-name.netlify.app/admin`
2. You'll see a login screen
3. Click **"Log in with Netlify Identity"**
4. Enter your email and password (the one you created from the invite email)
5. Click **"Log in"**

---

## üì∑ Adding a New Project (Album)

### Step 2: Create a New Project

1. Once logged in, you'll see the admin panel
2. In the left sidebar, click **"Photo Gallery"**
3. Click **"Projects"**
4. Click the big **"New Projects"** button (usually green or blue)

### Step 3: Fill Out the Form

You'll see a form with these fields:

**Project ID** (Required)
- Type a unique name like: `project7`, `project8`, `project9`, etc.
- Make sure it's different from existing projects
- Use lowercase letters and numbers only (no spaces)
- Example: `project7` or `summer2024`

**Title** (Required)
- This is the name that shows on your website
- Example: "Summer Fashion Series" or "Portrait Collection 2024"

**Description** (Required)
- Write a few sentences about this project
- Example: "A collection of portraits showcasing natural beauty and elegance."

**Thumbnail Image** (Required)
- This is the photo that shows on the Work page
- Click the upload area
- Select a photo from your computer
- Wait for it to upload
- This should be one of your best photos from this project

**Project Images** (Required - at least 1)
- Click **"Add Projects"** to add more photos
- For each photo:
  - Click the upload area
  - Select a photo from your computer
  - Wait for it to upload
- You can add as many photos as you want!
- To remove a photo, click the trash icon next to it

### Step 4: Save Your Project

1. Scroll to the top of the form
2. Click the **"Save"** button (usually in the top right)
3. Wait a few seconds - you'll see "Saved" or a success message
4. **Done!** Your photos are now on the website!

---

## üè† Adding Photos to Homepage Gallery

### Step 5: Add to Homepage (Optional)

If you want a photo to show on the homepage:

1. In the left sidebar, click **"Photo Gallery"**
2. Click **"Homepage Gallery"**
3. Click **"New Homepage Gallery"**
4. Fill out:
   - **Image**: Upload a photo
   - **Title**: Name for the photo
   - **Description**: Optional description
   - **Project ID**: Link it to a project (e.g., `project1`, `project7`)
5. Click **"Save"**

## üí° Tips & Tricks

### Photo Tips
- **File size**: Keep photos under 5MB each for faster loading
- **Format**: JPG files work best
- **Quality**: Use high-quality photos for best results
- **Naming**: You don't need to rename files - the system handles it

### Best Practices
- **Thumbnail**: Choose your best/most eye-catching photo as the thumbnail
- **Order**: The first photo you upload will be the first one shown
- **Descriptions**: Write clear, descriptive text so visitors know what they're looking at
- **Project IDs**: Use simple names like `project7`, `project8` - they don't show on the website

### Troubleshooting
- **Photos not uploading?** Check your internet connection and file size
- **Can't save?** Make sure all required fields are filled
- **Changes not showing?** Wait 1-2 minutes for the website to update
- **Forgot password?** Use "Forgot password" on the login page
