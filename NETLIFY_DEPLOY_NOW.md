## Step 1: Sign Up for Netlify
1. Go to [netlify.com](https://netlify.com)
2. Click **"Sign up"** in the top right
3. Click **"Sign up with GitHub"** (this is the easiest way)
4. Authorize Netlify to access your GitHub account

## Step 2: Import Your Repository
1. Once logged in, you'll see your dashboard
2. Click **"Add new site"** â†’ **"Import an existing project"**
3. Click **"Deploy with GitHub"**
4. You may need to authorize Netlify (click "Authorize Netlify")
5. Find and select **"aliyah-photography-website"** from the list
6. Click **"Deploy site"**

## Step 3: Wait for Deployment
- Netlify will automatically build and deploy your site
- This takes about 1-2 minutes
- You'll see a progress indicator
- When done, you'll see "Site is live" with a URL like: `https://random-name-123.netlify.app`

## Step 4: Enable Identity (For Admin Panel)
1. In your site dashboard, click **"Site settings"** (top menu)
2. Click **"Identity"** in the left sidebar
3. Click **"Enable Identity"** button
4. Scroll down to **"Registration preferences"**
   - Set to **"Invite only"** (recommended - only people you invite can access)
5. Scroll down to **"Services"** section
6. Click **"Enable Git Gateway"** button
7. Click **"Save"** at the bottom

## Step 5: Invite Your Cousin
1. Still in **"Identity"** settings, click **"Invite users"** button
2. Enter your cousin's email address
3. Click **"Send invite"**
4. She'll receive an email to create her account

## Step 6: Access the Admin Panel
1. Go to: `https://your-site-name.netlify.app/admin`
2. Click **"Log in with Netlify Identity"**
3. Sign in with your account
4. You should see the admin panel! ðŸŽ‰
