About

Hi! I'm Aliyah and I'm a photographer based in Silver Spring, Maryland. My focus is on fashion and beauty portraits.

My work is heavily inspired by my South Asian Muslim heritage and upbringing, which I always aim to reflect in all of my photos. Through my lens, I strive to capture the beauty, elegance, and rich cultural traditions that have shaped my identity.

Each photograph tells a story, blending contemporary fashion with timeless cultural elements, creating images that celebrate both individuality and heritage.
